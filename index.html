Our Menu Book a Table

At Indian Flavours, we bring you the rich and diverse culinary heritage of India. Our chefs craft each dish with authentic spices and traditional techniques, ensuring a memorable dining experience.

  • Authentic Indian recipes from across regions.
  • Fresh, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.
  • A warm and inviting ambiance for family and friends.

From the fiery curries of the North to the tangy delights of the South, our menu is a journey through India's vibrant food landscape. We are committed to providing you with an unforgettable taste of India.

Chef's Special Biryani

A unique blend of aromatic basmati rice, tender marinated meat (or vegetables), and exotic spices, slow-cooked to perfection. A signature dish from our head chef.

Ingredients: Basmati Rice, choice of Chicken/Mutton/Paneer, Saffron, Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon, Fresh Herbs. Flavour: Rich & Aromatic.

Royal Thali

Experience a king's feast with our Royal Thali, a grand platter featuring a selection of our finest curries, bread, rice, and dessert.

Includes: Dal Makhani, Shahi Paneer, Butter Chicken (or Veg Curry), Naan, Rice, Raita, Salad, Gulab Jamun. Flavour: Diverse & Balanced.

Seafood Platter

A delightful assortment of grilled and curried seafood, showcasing the coastal flavours of India.

Includes: Tandoori Prawns, Fish Fry, Goan Fish Curry, Lemon wedges, Mint Chutney. Flavour: Tangy & Spicy.

Vegan Delights

A specially curated selection of plant-based Indian dishes, rich in flavour and wholesome goodness.

Includes: Aloo Gobi, Chana Masala, Vegetable Biryani, Roti, Salad. Flavour: Wholesome & Mildly Spiced.

Dessert Extravaganza

Indulge your sweet tooth with a grand assortment of traditional Indian desserts, perfect for sharing.

Includes: Gulab Jamun, Jalebi, Gajar Halwa, Kulfi, Kheer. Flavour: Sweet & Aromatic.
